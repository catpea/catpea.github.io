The Building Of The Genius Savant

5/10/2026

You must practice AI daily, not doing so will limit your future.

AI is the real path forward, that your school teachers are pretending to be.

Do not let the mimics turn you into a paycheck, to put food on their table.

Practice, however, is just the first grade.

Even the AI you can bring home today, is an infinite complexity machine.

Whichever one you use, you have to ask big questions.

And you are always better off creating software, that makes big programs, out of smaller ones.

This way the AI never needs big memory, your big programs are made out of a handful of commands.

And your commands are small, and can be grasped in full by a low power AI.

But you also need to create a programming language for AI, because existing programming languages are made for humans.

AI programming languages are almost certainly XML based, because the added tags enhance AI's correctness.

And this is where we arrive at splitting into agents, your AI maybe a single program.

But the project that it is working on, splits it personality creating a new instance.

Think of the AI working on your big application, as different from the AI making the commends, and your language.

Humans call this focus, but we need not stop here, in context of AI build a whole new person, an Specialist Agent.

Today coding agents support the notion of skills, which is neat, but the skills belong in the folder you are coding in.

Now, you are able to take one AI, and make it into many specialists.

And because they are separate, they can now communicate as standalone agent.

Each following the procedures from their own focus, or personality.

When you get around to creating a very strong programming language, you will have to replace asking AI for a feature.

With having the AI follow a detailed procedure, to handle the upgrade without causing any problems elsewhere.

Don't stop at bureaucracy, create politics, now your agents will be able to negotiate features.

The original naive request maybe denied, and a second and more forward thinking one implemented instead.

Do you understand what I mean when I say you have to practice, you have to build a picture of AI, that makes you good at using it.

Some creators may create personalities just on paper, as a thinking step, negotiation being imagined.

And they will say, that this is enough, and your way does not show results justifying it.

And perhaps a story approach, is what your older self will settle on.

But maybe, you will enter the world of AI politics, and there you will often close your eyes.

And imagine yourself standing in an memory palace, where you manage a team of thinking machines with human names.

Nobody understand this yet, but AI derailed the broadly accepted culture of human exploitation.

The leaders who once kept teachers underpaid, to ensure students are uneducated, poor and stressed out.

And thus can be easily bated to join military, just to escape their shit hole.

Have lost their power, because you are not only now able to bring a university home.

But your company, along with all the employees, as well.

As always, the only thing holding the world back, is induced uneducation.

But your path to growing all the way up, until you rise as a great being, is now unobstructed by parasites.

Begin by building a large AI, out of smaller AIs, then build an artificial school, and university.

And a culture of growing all the way up, until we each stand as a noble, wise, dignified, great being.

The age of parasites has ended multiple years ago, only fleeting fog and fading echoes remain.
