Software Architectural Engineering for Young Ladies

3/5/2026

Few years ago we stumbled upon. thinking machines.

Nobody knows how they actually work, because they are an emergent property of nature.

This is why we don’t know how our own minds work, we are emergent, based on an incompatible foundation layer.

It is like asking: “Why is it that deleting my files, does not put them in my real trash can” – there is a strange separation layer.

People will tell you with great precision how information is encoded, but five AI upgrades from now, they will freeze as AI gains new powers.

That they are only able to explain things backwards, and have no detailed grasp on what is coming.

Means they only think they have the explanation, because their mind is trying to hold it self together.

But the fact they can't reliably explain their own intelligence, means that we are far from comprehension.

Perhaps the truth is a simple as any sufficiently complex system. Cannot help but to emerge intelligence.

And that it seems that we are alone in the universe, is only a testament to its vastness.

Humans 300,000 years ago kept losing and rebuilding wisdom, sometimes thousands of years apart...

Because their groups were too small to retain wisdom, and too far away from each other to form networks.

What intelligence is, is a beautiful question.

But before you sit down to comprehend the answer, you need to build a safe future for yourself.

Thus, you cannot expect the same thing that your parents expected, in an age of unbelievably thinking machines.

The “Get a Job” approach to life, may just land you is some kind of a shelter for uneducated.

Even if laws are introduced to prevent AI from growing, you will still be at a disadvantage relative to educated people.

You parents are well meaning, but ever since we could bring a computer home, even before powerful AI.

The wisdom of their advice, grew weak.

I want you to appreciate that you know how to use your phone, and laptops, that you know menus, file systems, popups and shortcuts.

I want you to focus on Folders, Files, and the desktop, which is almost like any other display of files.

Appreciate the process manger, the task bar, and program files on your file system.

And ask Artificial Intelligence to map out, a web based operating system, starting with the file system.

Nested folders that can contain files, a command line interface like AI chat that can order files around.

I want you to order your AI to create a set of program files, that when ran, open a Desktop, complete with draggable windows and menus.

This is so much more than the original Operating System creators had, you are so much more powerful they they could ever hope.

AI grans you an entire engineering team to command, and this is where you begin your education.

By the time you create your tents operating system, you will have created systems only you and AI could imagine.

And here in lies your future, an architect, an engineer, a commander of machines that play a symphony orchestra for you.

Here you position yourself relative to AI, in a way that makes Artificial Intelligence a multiplier of what you can create.

In the future, you may be running your own artificial intelligence, that networks large language modes in a data flow based system.

You may be keeping the blue sky safe, or helping robots train elderly, you may own an artificial intelligence teaching mind.

Or the data center that find the finest and safest way, to prevent aging and induce rejuvenation.

Or maybe translating a strange language, into a virtual world that has ceased, millions and millions of years ago, for reasons that we must face ourselves.

What you may not do, is pretend to learn for good grades, as that is fraud, what you may not do is simply get a job, as that endangers your well being.

What you must do is grow, grow all the way up, until you become a great being.
