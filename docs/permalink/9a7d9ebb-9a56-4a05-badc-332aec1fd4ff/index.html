A Call For Young Power Programmers

6/16/2026

Artificial Intelligence is really powerful, especially when you use example projects to communicate with it.

The AIs can also create 3D user interfaces with three.js, there are barely any such projects out there.

One of the most significant multiples is building on top of AI, placing AI in a hermetic docker container and exposing openAPI.

And then building not just a user interface for it, but batch job processing.

This will help you scheduler hundreds of things, rather than one.

An example of that is song generation, don't just generate a song, generate an album.

Don't just write lyrics, don't just write lyric descriptions, tell the AI how many songs you need on what subject.

You can give your album project file to state of the art models, and they will expand it, in ways that humans are barely ready for.

And you or your local-AI customers will wake up to hundreds of songs that were never heard before.

If you ever get frustrated it means, you went to far, you need to re-think the project.

And now tell your AI to use it as an example, of what you are looking for.

When you talk to a programming AI, everything you say slowly reveals what you prefer.

Every time you talk to an AI, your chat must end with a programming preference.

Followed by research discovery summary, it is silly to think that all your research is not connected.

The fancy app you make two years from now will be greatly aided, by the research findings of today.

Visualization of complex AI generates software is of course critical, but it is best that it is not bolted on at the end.

You have to invent a framework that fits your capabilities, and that then can be easily visualized in 3D via three.js, VR or not.

When your framework structures your code for visualization, and AI writes the visualizer, the whole becomes a mere formality.

3D visualizations are intense, and incredibly overwhelming to ponder, being by searching for images with "futuristic user interface" query.

We must prepare our tiny monkey minds, for a future where software is created on the fly, as needed.

Imagine a driving map on your phone set in satellite mode, you can see at the edges as it is continuously downloading more.

That is how future applications will work, whatever you need will be built when you need it.

And if you run such a business, you will not have to convince your customers to pay as they go.

What would this look like, imagine your favorite app on your phone, pinch-in/zoom-out, and see all the other screens you can generate.

Finally, in your mind under the overwhelming information of toady, sits a key to an AI far more efficient than the chaos of today.

You don't talk to it by typing, but via subset of English you click, it is a minimal language built by today's neural AI.

This demands that you watch a play-through, of Sam & Max: Hit the Road and Day of The Tentacle.

Think of this as a WikiWiki Keyboard, as it continues branching out beyond the initial vocabulary in all the right spots.

The world you operate on is completely symbolic, it is a coherent representation of our world, but in text.

Inform 7 and MUD/MOO can show you how to build such worlds, aside from being symbolic they are perfectly structured.

Thus easily visualizes, or projected into 3D.

The intelligence here, is the same as the intelligence of workers, courts, corporations, and governments.

Everything is well scripted, everyone and every thing follows the script, or their process libraries.

You don't need a big AI, and more interestingly, the more little AIs you have the faster the world thinks.

The technology of today has unlocked everything for you, the only thing ever denied to you, will be boredom.
