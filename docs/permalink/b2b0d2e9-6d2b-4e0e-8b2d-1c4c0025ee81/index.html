The AI Craze

6/29/2026

With the aid of AI, I am creating advanced software across several directions.

Programming languages, symbolic artificial Intelligence, workout music generators, programming whips and harnesses.

But, to use the fruits of my looms, I have to climb down from my ivory towers.

So today, while working on advanced music generation, I game myself the old winamp.

The only mp3 player that worked on my Pentium 90, that had me march into a patent scam office with a design for an mp3 player.

Only beat by the people who already worked on digital music, and found mp3 to be what they need.

They were going to scam a teenager out of ten thousand dollars, I might have actually beat cd players with mp3 decoders by a while.

Today, I had AI put winamp in my hand, not on my scrren, I built it as a full-scrren tablet application.

And this, not my more advanced research, is where I declare the AI Craze is Open.

The heart and soul of the AI craze is very simple, you use big AI to make intelligent applications.

You copy big AI powers into small useful applications, this does not mean you have to create a miniature AI.

You can create or improve applications, that were too hard to work on even for a team.

A music player with a 10 week workout plan, that will teach both mind and body wisdom is precious.

It is even more precious if you put it on a pristine unused tablet and sell it as a personal trainer that you open and use.

Pay attention, there are multiple steps.

You use AI to clean a tablet from ads, you use an existing application and improve it its features.

And then you generate high quality content for it, such as music, or narrated books.

The hardest part is selling it, because some people can’t afford it.

Look how you are unencumbered by app stores, and fees, how it is just you, your beautiful product, and word of mouth advertising.

You can create comic books, interactive videos, workout programs, and even instructions on how to create such a business.

Or, like me, you can just enjoy the triumph of having created a marvel, and keep going up the ladder building up higher and higher.

Until you have something that is clearly irresistible, the AI craze driving force is having the power to bypass the middle man.

To clear the tablet, and build something new, with AI helping you at every step.

Giving you powers of large corporations which are crushed by their own weight, while you can dance a butterfly, and punch like a speeding train.
