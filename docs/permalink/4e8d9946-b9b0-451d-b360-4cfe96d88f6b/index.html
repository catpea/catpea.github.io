SHIFT

4/1/2026

Just like your parents don’t fully understand that times have changed, you do not understand that Artificial Intelligence has changed times.

A small claim is, that the shift occurred in the beginning of march 2026, but every moth marked a shift since AI became public knowledge.

We have to change our lives, alter the course of our education, the rise of thinking machines gives us all free hardworking employees.

AI is really good at creating computer programs, and that is where we all need to be headed next.

An argument can be made that at this point those of us who can’t write code, are 40 months tardy – yes, it is not what we need to hear.

Learning programming, is yet another challenge in life, but it is a challenge that responds to our earlier curse, world poverty.

How terrible and unfair that we should be born poor, and given s*it education by teachers acting like they know what they are doing.

Well, AI is the cure, this is the equalizer.

Any school not teaching programming first and foremost, is just in business of manufacturing more poverty.

How dare they, keep the language of the machines, away from the hands and minds of those who need to fix the world.

The moment you hear the words thinking machine, is the moment you let the alarm ring, let the phone ring.

You have to learn programming, how to effectively communicate, with what I sometimes call “thinking files”, local AI is 16BG or video ram.

Where do you go from there, is based on who you are.

If you like the gym make a complex Interval Timer, if you like computer games, bring back Pixel Art with fine Pixel Art Tools.

If you like Philosophy, bring back the dead, all of them.

And if you like adventure, make sure all other hikers have a private AI earpiece that will talk to them.

Artificial Intelligence is an Infinite Complexity Machine, and once you can talk about programming with it, the number of possibilities.

You were given a head start to begin learning programming, right where we could bring a reasonably powerful desktop computer home.

Now, this is a warning, maybe the only warning you will ever receive, learn programming, learn programming, learn programming.

You have a team of artificial programmers, patiently waiting for your instructions.

I repeat, learn programming, now, so that you may rise in this unfair world, unobstructed by poverty.
