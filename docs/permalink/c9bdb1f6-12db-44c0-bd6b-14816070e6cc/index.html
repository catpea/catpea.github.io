Getting Ready For High School

7/19/2026

The adults have been compromised by poverty, they destroyed schools for easy paychecks.

You are forbidden to memorize for grades, And your teacher will expect you to do it.

Your teacher must follow the curriculum, Or they will not get paid.

They will get you to move with their plan, At all cost, including giving you a passing grade.

You are not permitted to excuse your teacher, Even if she truly is a sweet and rare person.

She may be a victim, but that does not, grant her the right to cheat you out of education.

Real education is like learning programming, You go to a class, take out your notebook.

And the teacher shows you how to read and write files, You close your notebook, seeing it as precious.

You go home, and from memory, You start opening and saving files in your apps.

From memory, because you have learned something you need.

Something you instantly use is real education, Something you learn for later is fake education.

Learning abstract concepts in physics, Works the same way.

Abstract is not an excuse, To not learn useful things you need.

What it asks of you is a road you take, But that road must be of personal interest to you.

To give example of entering a road that interests you, Other than programming.

Imagine hearing about lense grinding, And spending the summer safely making your own telescope.

Then daydreaming about the hand that holds the soldering iron.

Of the first signal, That reaches earth.

If you have no interest in some of the the many facets of Astro Biology.

If you prefer electronics, microcontrollers, Or using a wall projector to paint realistic paintings.

Stay away from physics, because you can’t learn it, You can only ruin it.

Imagine learning about saving files, And not having an interest in computers.

That is not an education, That is a psychological assault.

The teachers will be doing that to you, So that they can bring a paycheck home.

They will even use shame, to manipulate you, they may get others to laugh at you, to soften you.

And they will use grades and GPA to threaten you, By making it clear they can destroy your future.

They may use tactics such as giving meaningless awards, To increase instruction following.

All of this is assault, All of it perfectly tolerated.

Sometimes kids get so mixed up and stressed from this, They sign up for military.

Some don’t come back, Most return mixed up even more.

Brainlessly becoming a killing machine, Or even a dying machine does not make a hero.

It is assault, betrayal, it is an attack, It is the mental illness of the failed adults.

Be aware that there is a class of people who teach the road, They are called science popularizers.

They are the rare, the few, the flawed, The real teachers.

You have to become a teacher, the rule is that in their absence you must.

You have to build narrated books, You have to help others hear first.

One of the worst things you will discover, Is that your parents are on their side.

You don’t get to stay home, You must go to school by law.

You have to jump the hoops, Or be called a failure.

And the first hoop you jump, Will devastate your learning ability.

Once you switch to being told what to do, Hello teacher tell me what my lesson.

You become a farm animal, And you will be farmed.

I called my schools, Meat processing plants.

I was learning programming, so I did not care.

And I rose plenty enough, But it only ends in a failing grade.

When I drooped out, They tagged me as gifted.

But I am no more gifted than you, We are all learners.

I just resisted the assault, With class, with dignity, with courage.

The teachers who made my classmates laugh, Never told them to stop.

I have met red faced beasts, I can still recall them.

This is what resistance looks like, It is either this of the pasture.

People will try to tell you, That balance education is part of a system.

But that system is just parasites, Competing for money.

It tricked down to a assault, That people in their niceness decorate.

The schools never worked, Because of how incredibly effective they are.

When done right, when you get paid for learning and entrepreneurship.

As an investment, Into the future.

The world rises from processing and manufacture, From assault and killing, to greatness.

Your teachers are indirectly, made poor on purpose.

They are made to disrupt your real education, Because nobody needs you to become a great being.

Do not trust your friends not to flake out, Give them permission to fail and blend back in.

Do not forget that many a drowning victim, in panic, also took the person next to them.

You have free Libraries, Internet, and Artificial Intelligence.

Begin building the first authentic school You don’t have to be a teacher.

But you must teach that to grow up, Means to grow all the way up.

Until we each stand, as wise great beings.
