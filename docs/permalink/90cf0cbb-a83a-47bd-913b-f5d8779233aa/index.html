Bodybuilding Is Easy; But, Unbelievable

4/30/2026

Bodybuilding is is not about how heavy you lift, because lifting heavy means not lifting for long enough, and not lifting heavier.

Bodybuilding it is about rhythmically lifting non-stop, for the duration that you can afford to be at the gym until your body adapts.

30 minutes is correct, 45 minutes is perfect, three hours is not good.

And the body grows as fast as your leg muscles did, that one time you tried jogging.

Or as fast as astronauts regain their musculature, back on Earth.

It takes about a year, it you do it wrong it will take 15.

You have to gently challenge your body, so that it adapts and then just do it again.

And you have to be wise about it, don’t just start lifting, jog with 3lb dumbbells for 30 minutes.

Then when you can do it non-stop, run into a gym and start dancing wit them.

Slow jogging, of course converts doing anything with dumbbells, even just carrying the, to a full body exercise.

Dancing, twisting and turning, as you can safely now lift over your head, and do bicep curls... affect even more muscles.

Reasonable abdominal muscles, it turns out, are a byproduct of dancing with dumbbells.

It is true, they happen on their own, while stabilizing your body during dancing with dumbbells.

Bodybuilding is like jogging, but when you run the course non-stop strait through:

Instead of trying to beat your best time, you add more weight and start training your body again to run the full course.
