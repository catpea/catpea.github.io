Grow Strong My Comrade by Will Durant

3/21/2020

Grow strong, my comrade… so that you may stand
Unshaken when I fall, so that I may know
The shattered fragments of my song will come
At last to a finer melody in you;
So that I may tell my heart that you begin
Where passing I leave off and fathom more.
