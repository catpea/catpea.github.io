Grow Your Own Local Super Intelligence: It Is Just Step By Step Procedures In Text Files

6/7/2026

Your local AI is probably smart enough to become a super intelligence, Because all that a super intelligence is a library of text files with procedures.

So the intelligence that ships with a laptop that can follow your instructions, Can also follow instructions from a procedure file stored in well named folders.

The big AIs that you pay money for come with a time limit, You can’t use them to create big things, but you can use them to create procedures.

And they should use the internet, and you should provide them with tutorials you like, And the result is one clear procedure after another.

A library of these, becomes a super intelligence.

Because it is a language model, It is just text files.

—

Bigger AI does not mean better AI, you are just one customer out of many, These AIs will be instructed to do as little as possible to meet your needs.

—

There is more to these procedures, Because like anything precious, you can turn this into an art.

If you make the steps into stand-alone tasks, You can then ask AI to write a program that summons may AI to your laptop to solve them.

If you make tasks clearly testable, our come up with automatic tests, Your local AI can fail, lean, improve, and try again, gently updating its approach.

—

This is all you need to know, local AI, becomes super intelligent with addition of procedures.

Some made by you, Most made by big AI.

And yes this is the data AI companies are likely focusing on, They collect successful procedures and roll them into the compressed brains.

The thing about your local setup is, you want your AI to know the right things, And to do things the right way.

So you don’t ask it to make a plan and follow the steps, You ask big AI to make a procedure, look over it, make it perfect.

Then you save the procedure to your hard drive in a reasonable folder tree, And tell your local AI: here are the procedures you can follow.

It is just text files, a library of specific procedures, including library maintenance procedures, research procedures and library expansion.

To put it simply, use the Big AI, to make your little AI even bigger.
