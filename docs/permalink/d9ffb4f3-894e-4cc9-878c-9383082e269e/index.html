The Return Of Morgana And The Knights Of The Shattered Round

6/17/2026

Human beings have super powers, meta powers even.

Most of them lay dormant, because most humans lay dormant.

One of our superpowers is what I call the pantheon, it is a memory palace like mechanic.

As you can imagine, the wise make the character, and the stupid always try to break them.

Today, what remains of the legends is eroded, largely by religions.

To quote Merlin, "It is the doom of men that they forget."

Stained my monks that promise reward after death, for a lifetime of servitude, and call you a sheep.

Or a pig, if you are out on the islands, where there are no sheep.

Morgana and her brother King Arthur, are a memory palace for crafting great beings.

Great beings, only call for other great beings to rise, and she shepherds want to keep you a farm animal.

Next time you hear “Historians largely agree”, you might want to ask, were they religions – first.

Not all pantheons are stained, and some give birth to other knights.

Sir Galahad, was supposed to slay the dragons.

Had dragons not promised you can lay dormant, he would have finished the job.

But Morgana and Arthur, are of different ilk.

They are creatures of Valor, Honor, Dignity, and Decency, they are the true Sol Invictus.

All you need to restore the pantheon, is remove the inner conflict, the stains, the religion, and the lies.

The second coming, is the notion that each age shifts, and the second coming is the adaptation for the shifted age.

The second knights, unstained, in full dignity, continue their greater quest, teaching us, to be like they are.

No one expect you to be Morgana, Galahad, or Arthur, but you must earn your knighthood, your genius, your armor ans shield.

Only when you good blade carves the casques of men, only when your tough lance thrusteth sure.

Can you have the strength of ten, as your heart becomes pure.

Human beings are not servants, they can be tricked into serving.

But we are each meant to stand as an breakable great being, not a mimic, not a pretender, not a Green knight, nor a fool.

These aren’t just songs that I have created, every hero you have ever heard of has thousand faces.

And every story has a shape, to push along the archetypes, so that we may see what we can learn.

To awaken the legends, to awaken Morgana, you need two more things, Wisdom and Song.

By the power of Joseph Campbell Archetypes, And Kurt Vonnegut Shapes Of Stories.

With the help of the Philosopher who spake, "Without music, life would be a mistake."

And with wisdom and song, and by the power vested in me, in no small part by Artificial Intelligence.

Avalon stirs back to life, as Castle Camelot, slowly re-appears in Arthur’s realm.

But this time, we begin with the Grail in hand, it turned out to be a cup of a different kind of king.
