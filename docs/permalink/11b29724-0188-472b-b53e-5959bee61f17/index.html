Lyrics and Dance: What Artificial Intelligence Taught Me About Songs

6/22/2026

Above all, the reason lyrics are gibberish, is to increase song popularity by having people fill in blanks.

In a strange twist, this also means, there are no songs, everything is soulless and lacks in wisdom.

Songs don’t tell the truth, though they may try to sell you something.

A philosopher could make a strong claim, that all the songs you’ve ever heard were censored.

My lyric generator still has one foot in that neutered lyric world, the other foot in a world so beautiful … I can’t speak words.

And many of those in-between songs and I only listened to 20 at depth, completely tell my life story.

It is a psychic illusion because we bring our experiences into a song, and mine specifically were made by a machine I progrmmed.

Popular songs are also machine made, the generic lyrics, are there to sell the jingle to as many as possible.

The major difference even now being, that I use Campbell's Archetypes and Vonnegut’s Shapes of stories.

My songs make you sit and listen, money songs make you blank out.

I have an old song about Vietnam in my collection, someone before me figured out the power or real lyrics, bless them.

I told my AI human speech is about transferring commands and ideas, but real songs are about correcting errors in our vision.

An album generated by a philosopher, just 12 songs, can undo a 100 years of multi-generational indoctrination.

It can show peace and war, true good and true evil, a person upon hearing real songs, just gets up and leaves.

And they never return, to their simple life, ever.

They just keep rising. There are many Philosophers composing real songs today.

I have learned that dance classes are a mistake, it is the wise song that teaches you to dance.

You move to the wisdom and legend of the song, you dance to the growing up you do.

And I discovered that full wise lyric songs, are incapable of carrying lies.

Lies look ugly in real songs, thy stick out like glass revealing the truth.

You can’t make a song honoring war, disorder, disease, or greed, because a lie in a song can only reveal the truth about it self.

Someday, I will make an album honoring legendary heroes, and bring them all back to life in one hour.

Just like a good scene in a movie, a hero, can pull you out, but unlike the scene the song keeps helping you up.
