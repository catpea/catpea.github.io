Child, don’t join the parade of twisted souls, you’ve got something better, something beyond their dying voices. And through the ages, the flames burned bright, the intellect was the torchbearer, a love for wisdom, not as a prize, but as a guiding star in the blackened sky. The builders of the future, who stood at the threshold of the unknown, lifting humanity towards a vision of light.

These stepping stones, carved by the hands of those who saw beyond the veil, cannot be tainted by the whispers of fools, nor defined by the marks on a map. This is your world, this is the key, the crack in the wall, just waiting for you to unlock it.

The journey starts with the earth beneath your feet, with the rivers that wash away the filth of the mind, and the books—the sacred texts of those who lived true, who loved wisdom, life, and authenticity.

Let the scientists tell you your name, but never forget that you are a creature of the stars. Let the philosophers show you the path, but let nature heal you from the burdens of modern times, the weight of poverty, of work, of the constant noise of fools who never know the quiet of true thought.
