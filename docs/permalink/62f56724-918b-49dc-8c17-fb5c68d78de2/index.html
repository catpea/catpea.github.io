Learn Programming, Or Get Programmed

3/22/2026

Times have changed, the discovery of thinking machines, means the future has arrived, pretty much all at once.

Learning programming, is about putting you in-charge of machines, so that the machines are never put in-charge of you.

You learn programming privately, by asking AI open heart questions, it is a program that will never disappoint you, or judge you.

Programming, believe me, is one of the funnies things you can do, it has mascots like Gnus, Penguins, Bunnies, and even the Devil!

Computers were never too hard, but when you got stuck, the answers were in books, or on stuck-up internet forums.

Today the answers come from a digital friend, who can understand, who can think with you, and who will take you on learning quests.

It has never been too hard to learn programming, but it has never been easier.

There are discoveries to be made, but also paths not taken, this weekend I structured my applications based on a files system.

And then created files based on state machines, traffic light are a state machine, they just don't go wrong.

So you write a state change request to a control file: "go-red", and it emits color now yellow, waits, and finally goes red.

The files in the file-system I designed are signals, you subscribe, they give you their value, and keep you updated.

AI designs the state machines for me, they are like poems, by hand it would take teams of developers, AI does it in minutes.

I am not sorry to say that you can't ignore AI and programming, you are not a worker, teachers only trick you into bad grades.

You are meant to grow all the way up, until you become a great being, in an age of AI, you may just end up learning to no end.
