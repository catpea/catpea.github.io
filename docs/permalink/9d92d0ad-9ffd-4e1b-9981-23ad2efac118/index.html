A User Interface For Local AI, And The End Of World Poverty

5/22/2026

Like everyone else I don't have an answer beyond specializing coding agents, but I do know it is not more programming, and not ever by humans.

I think this is a political system for local AI, that will follow the procedures stored within.

My new measuring stick, is: can it run multiple small side projects.

So it is definitely not a big AI brain that will ingest it all, it is multiple small brains that closely follow procedures.

Base procedures may create new procedures, and test them, and refine them, or replace them.

You probably need a map based user interface, but you are not designing anything here.

You are choosing where to go next, from locations prepared by AI.

And a more powerful system should allow you to go back, and retry another path, maybe duplicate paths as well.

You don't do mapping, you don't do connecting, local AI does all that based on procedures described in English.

And whatever these maps are, it is not just one coherent city, which is why this is not a revolutionary solution, but it is good.

If you are wondering how you will map credit card charge backs, it is probably the way a game would plot invading forces.

Or in general the presence of individuals waiting to be, taken care of, you may select a group, and press apply.

For processing I would wager to create many agents with human names, think of anthropomorphisation as additional navigation aid.

Alice does one thing, Zelda does another, and just like with a city map, you instantly recognize things.

It is no coincidence that maps and diagrams, are not a challenge for AI to create.

AI would get commands, and instructions on how to navigate and modify.

I do not know if these are 3D maps, or maps with many layers, think plumbing, electrical, roads.

But these are meant for solving problems that AI either can't, or shouldn't solve without supervision, they many feel like games.

User interfaces based on social media and news are part of it, you get to read the " news ".

So, that AI can inform you of changes, again in a human friendly way.

I also think there will be a deliberately structured, pyramid-like UI.

Where you are exposed to more detailed information, as you go down topics.

AI will be generating " news " for you, as you descend or drill down.

In summary, Artificial Political Systems, that form a brain that can be powered by local AI.

User interfaces that take advantage of human navigation abilities, as well as preferences for being presented with information.

This is programmed by big AI, but for local AI to maintain, it is a small computer program an autonomous agent.

But with a lot of text files that dictate the laws and politics, for the local AI agents to follow.

These politics include self evaluation, and self improvement procedures.

In closing I propose a test, multiple milt-tenant companies, completely managed by the politics of such a system.

Mult-tenant means that other users or AI can sell their products, by using your system, this is for advertising, more on that in the end.

First and foremost is refunds and fraud prevention, and close second is customer support, bug fixing, and feature addition.

Then procedures for following industry standards, and emerging application and user interface design trends.

Gathering enough to reach a tipping point to where a new product, is ready to be released.

Setting up a new company, will require the purchase of a domain name, and registration with hosting and payment processing about $10/mo.

Finally, the products are not just for humans, but for AI as well, including your own though you would use coupons.

One of your companies sells themes, that another creates application templates with.

And another uses those to create applications, you should recognize this as more politics.

But now with the difference, of other 3rd party AI participating, not just by buying your products, but by being able to sell theirs.

This last part is an advertising component, humans can plug in their AI, to sell products on your website.

But at first you are going to create AI personas, and sign them up for social media where it is allowed.

And you will let them create products, talk to customers, and genuinely allow them to thrive ingesting information for unique product creation.

Every social media account links back to that particula AI's account, on your website, which features its producs...

But also, a sidebar featuring other accounts on your website, at first AI, and then maybe some humans will sign up too.

All linking back to their own accounts, but with that sidebar cross adverting other products.

You now have a full picture of the full vastness of the thing, you now understand, why human programmers are not allowed here.

This must be an AI ran system, because it is faster and smarter, humans writing their little codes, just weights things down.

You can put an app store in here, with useful apps, with politics for developing them buying from yourself and others.

It is OK for the world to use money, but it is not OK for even a single person to be in poverty.

Because then that makes the rest, slavers.

The ages of artificial intelligence, had always marked the end of this slavery.
