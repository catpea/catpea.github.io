Use Artificial Intelligence To Build And Sell Useful Applications

6/10/2026

If a model can follow your directions, It can follow directions from a file.

That means the intelligence is in the text file, All you need is a thinking machine, the rest are directions.

The huge models have the advantage of good instincts, But guess what, you can put instincts into a file.

Big companies have to serve everybody, You only need instructions for a specific purpose.

Ask the big AI for help with something specific, Ask your little AI to remember the steps.

—

Instincts are useful in a pinch, But you want auditable procedures for real work.

—

Think of large language models are expensive for us, But also expensive to run, don’t count on big companies.

You can rent a huge GPU for $7 an hour, Load a massive model, and use a harness to take what you need.

I am still in local AI research phase, I asked AI for a model switching server with remote support.

But I don’t think I will need it, Because I focused on a single domain User Interface components.

It is a programming language, one of many I made, That I am refining by translating old code into new.

—

When you think about it, You won’t need a big model either, not for long.

Because this day and age integrating AI is useful, But it is all about massively advanced and high quality applications.

I will pay you for useful things that I can own, And update, preferably on my own, and lower complexity of my work.

This is how business work, this is why startup advisors say: Find a niche market, this is exactly how it works.

—

I selected, photo editing and image processing, video editing, audio and music generation generation and composition.

We use all these programs all our lives, We know a lot about them.

If you choose to assemble a library of user interface components first, All you need is to broadcast action to and from your program.

It is called event sourcing, to constantly update a value, You use signals that monitor for events with specific names.

There is no other way, because the other ways do not come, With, undo, redo, history, replay, restore, and logs.

User interface components can ruler out what they con’t care about, An event has a name like “invert folors”.

But give it a channel as well, And just have components listen or multiple or particular channels.

Events, Components, Applications.

—

One thing about software that is special, Is that it requires no warehousing, you sell copies.

And now more true than ever, It requires no upfront investment, you just use ai.

You don’t have to advertise, You just have to show how powerful and beautiful your program is.

—

I think the most fun project is the local AI, That does all the maintenance and assembly of the applications you sell.

You will learn all the lessons you need for the future, And if you do it right…

You will get to say: “And I never wrote a line of code.”

Do not let schools trick you that grades or GPA matters, Only results matter.

New knowledge that only lifts you higher and higher, So that you may grow all the way up, and become a great being.
