Demand Authentic Education

Wednesday • February 25th 2026 • 9:58:55 pm

In the age of Artificial Intelligence, for a dollar a day, you can host a team of five or more programmers in your home.

The number of your Artificial Intelligence programmers, is only limited by the number of your ideas.

Great software ideas are hard to begin with, but without knowledge of programming they are almost impossible.

At the very least, this pristine and flawless, dog and pony of High School must teach programming.

Because in the age of thinking machines, inability to program is a real disability.

Artificial intelligence exposes standardized education, as a real cruelty, that sentences brilliant minds to poverty.

Whatever is coming will not end in more betrayals, and more violations, that is not sustainable.

The future will demand what every human being is capable of, unique, exceptional abilities.

Brought on the only way that excellence can be reached, by years, or even decades of authentic and meaningful education.

Teach the students that every class must end with new abilities, just like each programming class enables with new programming powers.

Coercing students to pretend to learn, by presenting fake GPA, as the next gateway in life is a betrayal and a crime.

Dismissing such letters with "what is he going on about", is a clear admission of guilt, and perhaps madness.

Fake education and impossible demands, that contradict the promise of growing up and learning .

Do not just destroy trust in schools and teachers, they drive the betrayal home.

As parents demand good grades, something that is not possible unless the student pretends to learn.

Students, it is rarely a good idea to blindly participate in theate, you may just be defeating yourselves in a war you don't understand.

You are meant to grow all the way up, until you become a great being!

Demand authentic education, or watch your future shrink to poverty and then nothing at all.

Do not pretend to learn, stand up for meanigful knowledge and wisdom, reject smallness - for in it - you may just witness your future's end.
