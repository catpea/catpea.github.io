Do Not Underestimate Your Power

4/15/2026

It is all very nice when I convince you, that you are as smart as Newton, Darwin, Einstein, Hawking, Leonardo DaVinci.

It is easy to snap you out of your “My grades weren’t that good” slumber, your horrifying teachers indoctrinated you into.

Once you see it, you naturally resist it, especially because their reasons for it, was just pretending to know what they were doing to sell you out for money.

But until now it was a challenge to hep you to the start of your spiral, the upward winding pathway that revolves around your curiosities.

It seems so trivial to start with stars, or model planes, or dance, but before you know it you have dipped your toes…

in Astro-Biology, speculative thought. Like how do they hold the soldering iron to make that radio.

Model planes, snaps so swiftly into 3D printing, 3D modeling, and then milling, and CNC, and gcode,

And before you even notice procedural modeling, and shockingly and with a whiplash generative modeling.

And the yet to be discovered, Symbolic Generative Modeling, that you stop yourself, and genuinely ask: Do I need a vacation?

Let me be the one to answer: always.

And dance, this silly thing, that your mom tells you not to do, because you’ll knock everything over…

Becomes anti-aging and rejuvenation research, and now a real nuisance, in your mom’s life, as now she’s one of your research subjects.

And in deed, aging is a corruption or decay, and fitness or dance, do bring you back out, he way physical therapy restores a once hurt patient.

As colorful as challenging, as meaningful as this genius journey was, it was rather slow, because you had to take nearly every step.

With Artificial Intelligence, that is now becoming optimized enough, to even somewhat run on a phone: you have staff, programmers, researchers.

It is very disarming, when you realize, your true power today, you have to take a break from school, grades, misery, work, stress.

Take a walk in the woods, with AI.

Artificial Intelligence makes you a CEO, of a company, the fundamentals of your very existence just changed.

You have a research team, programmers, researchers, thinkers, philosophers, all waiting for your direction.

You need to move faster, you are already a head of a powerful corporation, or think tank, of software development company.

You don’t have time to work on your GPA, and get scammed out of your savings for a branded impression of education.

What job are you seeking, where are you going the old way, as someone who right now has the power of PhD level AI.

Let me close with a tiny challenge, find a company that you think is clever, and ask AI of your choosing to make a lightweight version.

To automate it with software, the workflows and responsibilities, the software and customer management.

And really look at what ti would take to instruct the AI, to make it solid and sound, and secure.

And then be ready, to head multiple such AI managed endeavors.

Once you build something sturdy, you cannot help but to add new features, that set you apart, that make your customers appreciate you.

And remember that you can move up, or back where instead of building, you sell the AI instructions that build it for you customers.

You can take another step, and now enable your customers, to run multiple such outfits, multiple AI managed companies.

And all you do is maintain the blueprints, you refine, research, test, upgrade, and ensure business success.

And just the moment you think to yourself, how can I possibly automate selling physical this or that.

You don’t have to, you use AI to create model designs, that then your customers use a manufacturing company for.

Heads up! You may not realize how powerful you are in the age of Artificial Intelligence.
