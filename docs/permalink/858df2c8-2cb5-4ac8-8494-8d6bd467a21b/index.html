You Finish Everything All The Time; Or, What Is It Like When You Are Finally Programming With AI?

7/9/2026

First of all, Gradpa and all the contrarians in the village are stirring, AI this, AI that, but none of them have written a line of code.

They don't wish you ill, they just don't like abrupt change, you are going to be a round for a lot longer than they will.

You are going to live in a worlds of robots running on the streets, on those stilt legs that make human runners faster.

A single food delivery robot from 10 years in your future, will change Grandpa's view.

But you can't wait 10 years, you have to be 20 years ahead of everyone, that is how a good business is ran.

You have multiple monitors, and many virtual desktops, on each desktop as many as 9 terminal screen - black hacer stuff.

In each terminal a state of the art artificial intelligence model, thinking with the power of a portable nuclear power generator.

There are CPUs and GPUs out there cooking their paint off, because of the work you are performing on your computer.

Lets talk about money first, you have the ability to upgrade human brains.

Think neural jack, think mem doubler, neural kit, you can hand your customer a WebXR url for VR headgear.

You don't have to make the sale, the first one is always free, the customer sets the price.

The price, sets the priority of the project.

The way your customer loads the information. is through hiking.

You send them to Springer Mountain in Georgia, and after two weeks, when their life resets.

Their brain enters a learning mode, and that is where you hit them with the payload.

Beautifully voiced custom made Audio Books, artificial voice but more profound than any human.

Energy generating songs across genres, that continue the teaching.

And if they care for it, and I would, the VR package.

After about 9 weeks camping, and exposure to the neural kit.

Which also includes standard audio books, written by real authors.

They will make the required changes in their life, and likely request another upgrade.

This is the world you live in, before you know it life extension drops.

The world will transform in ways, that are hard to comprehend.

The worst that can happen, is that you will only live a 160 years.

But it is ridiculous or even stupid to think like this, because in a 140 years life will be extended indefinite.

We've been performing AI led genetic research with cell on chip, for multiple years now, presumably in secret.

And as you are no doubt learning now, just like the AI mind that works wonders.

We don't need to comprehend the cure in full, it just needs to reliably work.

There is no shortage of people willing to test extension, there is no negative, for someone who has reached their end.

You will have your money, and you will have a very long life ahead of you.

Our futures are tightly knit with, with these magnificent thinking machines.

But the work you will be performing, is the work of a CEO.

You will run over 100 terminal windows across 10 desktops, that represent 10 of your top projects.

And you can only really talk to the AI, the tasks it is performing are far too complex to hand manage.

The AI will be in your home, you may tap into a community nuclear generator.

And eventually, not so long from now, you will have options as to where to move.

Genetic engineering won't stop with the cure of aging, you will remain mostly human.

But your adaptions, will give you and your family options.

I don't think we will stray very far, but when you do the math, it won't take long to colonize the milky way.

It is rather suspicions, that it hasn't been colonized already.

In closing, I must repeat the warning, honor your older self, by learning programming with AI as soon as possible.

Do not pretend to learn, evey moment counts, think, judge, evaluate, learn, rise.

Your quest for wisdom only seems optional, because we have been rather impressively domesticated.

But we are not livestock, we do not belong in a farm.

Pull your self together, and become an independent great being, so that you can efficiently operate a powerful local AI.

Do it now, do not delay, do not let simple people stand in your way.
