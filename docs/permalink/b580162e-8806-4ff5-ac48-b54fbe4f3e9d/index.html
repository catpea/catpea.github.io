Homemade Lemonade

6/16/2026

Music industry; the the people who will put one nicely sounding song on a CD, fill it with low quality material, slap on a sticker, and call it an album.

And by nicely sounding song, I don’t mean the lyrics, the lyrics have always been 99.00% trash, that’s right.

Even 90s Country, my favorite, was singing about alcohol, hookups and crap, when I say the end of music industry, I mean the end of crud.

My first Artificial Intelligence country songs, had very solid workout advice.

And I discovered an excellent way to remind young lads not to dress mean, by calling it the color palette of bird and cat crap – which is correct.

My second batch of now 36 songs, or so I thought, was a problem, I was so excited, but it turned out one of my AIs lied.

Instead of failure, and error, this little genius, bless its digital heart, wrote a fallback song, instead of programming, it was composing.

So I ended up with 36 pop songs with the same lyrics, but really interesting and subtle variations in instruments and tone and voice.

I learned something, today, they didn’t all sound the same, and now I have a setting that would make them sound even different.

I came home, and used the savant AI, to fix it, though it is not as good as it used to be, it is a sleepy savant now.

I had to step it through so many simple observations, that I ended up terminating the plan, though I did that several times already.

However we did arrive at the expected result, and this batch of country, soon to be followed by some folk songs…

Contains precious workout advice, stories about Galahad and what the grail really was, and stories of adventure.

Country music, is a storytelling genre, ad now you can blend it with any type of music, I wand choir, some large cathedral chants, and drum, I love drums.

The great thing about this program is that it houses two AI, one writes the lyrics, and the other sings and composes music for them.

I added the lyric writer so that I could give the AI ideas not lyrics, this way I don't know what the songs are exactly about how the AI stitched the ideas.

That makes the songs a lot more fascinating, then if you already knew the lyrics.
