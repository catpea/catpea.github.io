School Is Out: A Quick Note About Programming With AI

7/18/2026

For the first time, in my rather amazing life.

For a short moment it occurred to me, I might have wasted a decade of my life.

That is how overwhelming programming with AI is, It will check you, it will make you rethink.

I needed the decade in question, My programming became extraordinarily complex.

The code was small and simple, The programs were not.

What made me question it, Is creating of an example program called ffstudio.

It is a program the AI was working on, Just to improve my programming language.

In that moment of weakness I just realized this is impossible without AI.

AI gives me a team of, about 12 developers.

But there are no humans, that would want to work for me.

Working for me is soul crushing, There are no milestones just a raw power chase.

The backend to ffstudo, is the industry standard ffmpeg command.

Everything you need to know about it, Is that it’s visual programming language is in text.

I understand it perfectly, But there are automatic shortcuts by omission.

That make the language, Challenging.

—

I know of maybe five programs, that tried to do what I did.

There maybe ten in existence, Across different languages.

But none of them were created, Without any effort, and as a code example.

This is the heart of what made me realize, That real programming was impossible before.

—

The key point to understand here, Is that you now have a team.

It can be as many as 20 machine people working for you, Non stop, no sleep, local AI trained by state of the art.

AI had bloomed so much this year, That by contrast High School will destroy you.

Not learning the art of creating Artificial Intelligence, Is guaranteed to keep you in poverty.

To put it the other way, The chain of poverty that turned humanity into a farm is not broken.

—

The only reason we are all still staying around, Is because that is how farm animals behave at first.

They have been so stuck, they became crazed in a sleepy way.

—

It is hard to say where and when to start, Create a programming language like I did.

Or wait until local AI becomes super smart, So that you don’t have to pay monthly fees.

I heard a mom at the supermarket explain t her daughter, That she has to go to school to learn.

But the truth is that you don’t learn in school, You only pretend to learn so that it does not ruin your life.

AI is now a way out of this nightmare, You must learn how to use it.

You have to know to ask for Zooming User Interfaces, Visual Programming Languages, Custom Language Agents.

That complex decade of mine helped me rise above the average things AI effortlessly makes.

You must make the same climb, The school is out.
